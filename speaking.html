IELTS 12 - Writing Practice

Test 1

Writing Task 1

You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.

The bar chart below shows the percentage of Australian men and women in different age groups who did regular physical activity in 2010.

Summacarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features and make comparisons where relevant.

write at least 150 words.

Percentage of Australian Men and woman doing regular physical activity: 2010

Writing Task 2

Write about the following topic:

Some people believe that it is good to share as much information as possible in scientific research, business and the academic world. Others believe that some information is too important or too valuable to be shared freely.

Discuss both views and give your opinion.

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevante examples from your own knowledge or experience.
